# Breakfast Recipe: Sweet & Savory Delight

## Mood: Something sweet and something savory

In this breakfast recipe, we'll create dishes that balance sweet and savory elements while adhering to your desired macros of 20g fat, 50g carbs, and 30g protein.

### Ingredients

**Savory Dish: Creamy Kale & Mozzarella Scramble**  
- Kale: 100g (3.3g)  
- Mozzarella: 100g (3.1g)  
- Cheddar: 40g (0.32g) for a more intense flavor  
- Lentils: 100g (3.8g)  
- Olive Oil: 1 tbsp (14g fat)  

**Sweet Dish: Blueberry & Peanut Butter Toast**  
- Crackers (or bread): 100g (20g carbs)  
- Peanut Butter: 20g (10g fat, 5g protein)  
- Blueberries: 100g (14g carbs)  

### Total Achieved Macros
- **Total Fat:** 14g (olive oil: 14g, mozzarella & cheddar, peanut butter)  
- **Total Carbs:** 38g (carbs from crackers, blueberries)  
- **Total Protein:** 25g (lentils, mozzarella, cheddar, peanut butter)  

### Instructions

#### Creamy Kale & Mozzarella Scramble

1. **Sauté the Kale:**  
   - Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.  
   - Add 100g kale, sautéing until wilted (about 3-4 minutes).  

2. **Add Lentils:**  
   - Stir in 100g of cooked lentils, cooking for an additional 2 minutes.  

3. **Add Cheeses:**  
   - Mix in 100g mozzarella and 40g cheddar until melted and thoroughly combined.  
   - Season with salt, pepper, and any herbs of choice (like oregano or basil).  

4. **Serve:**  
   - Plate your creamy kale and mozzarella mix.  

#### Blueberry & Peanut Butter Toast

1. **Prepare Toppings:**  
   - Spread 20g of peanut butter over 100g of crackers (or toast if using bread).  

2. **Top with Blueberries:**  
   - Scatter 100g of fresh blueberries over the peanut butter.  

3. **Serve:**  
   - Serve with a sprinkle of cinnamon over the top for added flavor.  

### Serving Suggestion
Pair the creamy scramble with your blueberry and peanut butter toast on the side. This combination provides a delightful balance of savory and sweet, while also aligning closely with your macros.

### Macro Breakdown
- **Creamy Kale & Mozzarella Scramble:**  
  - Fat: ~14g  
  - Carbs: ~10g  
  - Protein: ~16g  

- **Blueberry & Peanut Butter Toast:**  
  - Fat: ~10g  
  - Carbs: ~28g  
  - Protein: ~9g  

### Enjoy!
This tasty breakfast not only meets your dietary goals but also satisfies both your sweet and savory cravings. Enjoy your culinary creation!
